POWER BI DASHBOARD – HR ANALYTICS  
Dashboard Layout, Visuals, and Insights

1. DASHBOARD OVERVIEW
The HR Analytics Dashboard provides a complete view of employee demographics, attrition patterns, salary distribution, and performance insights.  
It helps HR teams and leadership understand workforce trends and make data‑driven decisions.

------------------------------------------------------------

2. PAGE 1 – EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Visuals:
- KPI Cards:
  - Total Employees
  - Total Attrition
  - Attrition Rate
  - Average Monthly Income
- Line Chart:
  - Attrition Trend Over Time (if applicable)
- Bar Chart:
  - Attrition by Department
- Donut Chart:
  - Gender Distribution
- Card:
  - Average Age

Insights:
- Sales department shows the highest attrition.
- Younger employees (under 30) leave more frequently.
- Average monthly income varies significantly across departments.

------------------------------------------------------------

3. PAGE 2 – ATTRITION ANALYSIS

Visuals:
- Stacked Bar Chart:
  - Attrition by Age Group
- Clustered Column Chart:
  - Attrition by Job Role
- Table:
  - EmployeeID, Department, JobRole, MonthlyIncome, Attrition
- Heatmap:
  - Attrition vs. Years at Company

Insights:
- Employees with 1–3 years at the company show the highest turnover.
- Sales Executives and Sales Representatives have the highest attrition.
- Job satisfaction plays a major role in retention.

------------------------------------------------------------

4. PAGE 3 – SALARY & PERFORMANCE INSIGHTS

Visuals:
- Box Plot:
  - Salary Distribution by Department
- Bar Chart:
  - Average Salary by Job Role
- Scatter Plot:
  - Monthly Income vs. Performance Rating
- KPI:
  - Highest Paid Department

Insights:
- R&D roles have higher average salaries.
- No strong correlation between salary and performance rating.
- HR roles show stable salary ranges with low attrition.

------------------------------------------------------------

5. PAGE 4 – WORKFORCE DEMOGRAPHICS

Visuals:
- Age Distribution Histogram
- Gender Breakdown Donut Chart
- Department Headcount Bar Chart
- Years at Company Distribution

Insights:
- Workforce is mostly between ages 28–45.
- Gender distribution is balanced across departments.
- Tenure distribution shows many employees in early career stages.

------------------------------------------------------------

6. RECOMMENDATIONS

Based on the analysis:
- Improve onboarding and early‑career support to reduce 1–3 year attrition.
- Review compensation structure for Sales roles.
- Conduct job satisfaction surveys for high‑attrition roles.
- Offer career development programs for younger employees.

------------------------------------------------------------

7. CONCLUSION

This dashboard provides a complete view of workforce health and HR performance.  
It demonstrates strong skills in:
- Data modeling
- SQL analysis
- Power BI dashboard design
- HR storytelling and insights

It is a valuable addition to a professional data analytics portfolio.
