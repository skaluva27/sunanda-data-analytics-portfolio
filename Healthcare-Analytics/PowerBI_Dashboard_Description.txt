POWER BI DASHBOARD – HEALTHCARE ANALYTICS  
Dashboard Layout, Visuals, and Insights

1. DASHBOARD OVERVIEW
The Healthcare Analytics Dashboard provides a complete view of patient operations, including wait times, no‑show patterns, department efficiency, and satisfaction levels.  
It helps healthcare administrators improve patient flow, reduce delays, and enhance service quality.

------------------------------------------------------------

2. PAGE 1 – EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Visuals:
- KPI Cards:
  - Total Patients
  - No‑Show Rate
  - Average Wait Time
  - Average Satisfaction Score
- Donut Chart:
  - Appointment Type Breakdown
- Bar Chart:
  - Patients by Department
- Line Chart:
  - Hourly Patient Load

Insights:
- No‑show rate is highest for new patient appointments.
- Morning hours show the highest patient volume.
- Orthopedics and Cardiology have longer wait times.

------------------------------------------------------------

3. PAGE 2 – WAIT TIME ANALYSIS

Visuals:
- Bar Chart:
  - Average Wait Time by Department
- Scatter Plot:
  - Wait Time vs. Age
- Table:
  - PatientID, Department, AppointmentType, WaitTimeMinutes
- KPI:
  - Longest Average Wait Time (Department)

Insights:
- Orthopedics has the longest average wait time.
- Younger patients tend to have shorter wait times.
- Follow‑up appointments generally move faster.

------------------------------------------------------------

4. PAGE 3 – NO‑SHOW ANALYSIS

Visuals:
- Stacked Column Chart:
  - No‑Show Count by Department
- Bar Chart:
  - No‑Show Rate by Appointment Type
- Heatmap:
  - No‑Show Patterns by Hour of Day
- KPI:
  - Total No‑Shows

Insights:
- New patient appointments have the highest no‑show rate.
- Mid‑afternoon appointments show more no‑shows.
- Pediatrics has the lowest no‑show rate.

------------------------------------------------------------

5. PAGE 4 – PATIENT SATISFACTION

Visuals:
- Bar Chart:
  - Average Satisfaction Score by Department
- Scatter Plot:
  - Satisfaction vs. Wait Time
- Donut Chart:
  - Satisfaction Score Distribution
- Table:
  - PatientID, Department, WaitTimeMinutes, SatisfactionScore

Insights:
- Satisfaction drops significantly when wait time exceeds 20 minutes.
- General Medicine has the highest satisfaction scores.
- Orthopedics shows room for improvement.

------------------------------------------------------------

6. PAGE 5 – TIME-BASED TRENDS

Visuals:
- Line Chart:
  - Patient Volume by Hour
- Column Chart:
  - Appointments by Day of Week
- Area Chart:
  - Wait Time Trend Over the Day

Insights:
- Peak patient load occurs between 9 AM and 11 AM.
- Fridays have the lowest appointment volume.
- Afternoon wait times increase due to backlog.

------------------------------------------------------------

7. RECOMMENDATIONS

Based on the analysis:
- Reduce wait times in Orthopedics through better scheduling.
- Implement reminder systems to reduce no‑shows for new patients.
- Add more morning appointment slots to handle peak load.
- Improve patient flow during afternoon hours to reduce delays.
- Monitor satisfaction scores closely for departments with long wait times.

------------------------------------------------------------
